Expect a reply within a few days. Frequently asked questions Does Minuted work offline? Yes, completely. Minuted doesn't make any network requests and works without internet. Where is my data stored? All data — your roster of people, meeting history, settings — stays on your device. It is never uploaded to a server or synced to the cloud. Why do I need an hourly rate for someone on salary? Minuted estimates hourly rate from annual salary by dividing by 2,080 (a standard working-hours-per-year figure). You can adjust this ratio in Settings. The cost in the Live Activity isn't ticking — is that a bug? No, this is intentional. iOS doesn't allow Live Activities to update custom numbers continuously. Minuted shows the elapsed time ticking and the burn rate as a static reference. Open the app to see the exact live cost. How do I reset all my data? Open Minuted, go to Settings (gear icon on the Roster tab), and tap "Reset all data." You'll be asked to confirm. This can't be undone. Can I back up my data? Not directly within Minuted. However, if you have iCloud backups enabled for your iPhone, your Minuted data is included in that backup per your iCloud settings. Will there be an iPad or Apple Watch version? Possibly in future updates. The current version is iPhone-only.
