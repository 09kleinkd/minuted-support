Privacy Policy Minuted · Last updated April 17, 2026 Summary Minuted does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All data stays on your device. What Minuted stores Minuted stores the following data locally on your device only: The names and hourly rates of people you add to your roster Records of meetings you run, including their duration and attendees This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to any cloud service, backed up to any server, or shared with any third party. What Minuted does not do No account or sign-in is required to use Minuted No analytics or tracking frameworks are included No crash reporting services are used No advertising or advertising-related data collection No data is shared with third parties Data deletion To delete your data, either use the "Reset all data" option in Minuted's Settings, or delete the app from your device. Deleting the app removes all associated data. iCloud and device backups If you have iCloud backups enabled for your iPhone, Apple may include Minuted's local data in your device backup per your iCloud settings. This data remains encrypted under your Apple ID and is not accessible to the developer of Minuted. Children's privacy Minuted is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children. Changes to this policy If this policy changes materially, an updated version will be posted here with a revised date.
